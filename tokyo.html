Things to note.

This grid system is super flexible. If you go over or under the 12 column layout, it'll adapt.

Because of the fixed layout, if you use two cell-4's, they'll adapt to cell-6's. If you add two more cell-4's they'll go back to cell-4's.

Nesting in this grid system is also super flexible. Refer to some examples above to see!
